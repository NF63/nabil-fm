Statistics and Sources

The problem

  • 747,560 households have problematic debts (~1.6M people) - CBS / Panteia 2024
  • 40% drop out before reaching debt settlement - NVVK Jaarverslag 2023
  • 13 creditors on average per household in debt - NVVK
  • 8 years average wait before seeking help - WRR / SchuldenLabNL
  • 56% feel shame about debt arrears - Panteia 2022
  • 31% say shame prevents seeking help - NIBUD Geldzorgenmonitor 2024
  • 11% reach rate - only ~82,000 reach formal assistance - NVVK / CBS
  • 551,000 people living in poverty in 2024 - CBS Armoedemonitor
  • EUR 130M/year government investment in debt interventions - Rijksbegroting SZW

The language barrier

  • 50% of Turkish-born pensioners don't claim AIO benefits they're owed, vs 15% Surinamese-born (language is the key variable) - NIDI, 2018
  • 12% of foreign-born Amsterdammers have problematic debt, vs 4% Dutch-background (3x rate) - Amsterdam Beleidsplan Schuldhulpverlening
  • 50.3% of schuldhulpverlening clients are at risk of low literacy - Kredietbank NL & Syncasso, 2018
  • 18.4% of people with non-Western migration background cite language as a barrier to financial services - AFM, 2021
  • 2.5 million adults functionally illiterate; 39.2% of working-age group has migration background - Stichting Lezen & Schrijven
  • ~600,000 people miss zorgtoeslag they're eligible for (EUR 515M unclaimed/year) - Panteia, 2021
  • ~175,000 households miss huurtoeslag (EUR 474M unclaimed/year) - Panteia, 2021
  • All CJIB fines, Belastingdienst letters, gemeente correspondence, toeslagen portals: Dutch only
  • Geldfit added translated info pages in 7+ languages, but warns "some sections may not be translated" - the core interactive tools remain primarily Dutch

Queen Maxima's advocacy

"Het is jouw schuld, maar het is niet jouw schuld" (It's your debt, but it's not your fault) - Queen Maxima, January 2026, calling for systemic change in how the Netherlands supports people in debt.

The Ombudsman

The Nationale Ombudsman published "DigiD helpt niet mee" (DigiD doesn't help) in December 2023, finding digital authorisation for legal representatives is "still not adequately arranged." Follow-ups in 2024 and 2025 expressed disappointment at lack of progress. Meanwhile, 2.5 million adults lack basic digital skills (CBS 2025) and 18,000 asylum seekers were waiting for a BSN just to get started (NOS 2024).

Architecture

Design principle: Claude reasons, code calculates

  • Claude handles: multilingual conversation, document vision (photographed letters), eligibility reasoning, plain-language explanations, formal letter generation
  • Deterministic code handles: all financial calculations - hardcoded 2026 thresholds from Toeslagenkaart, published formulas from Belastingdienst. No LLM arithmetic.
  • Why: LLMs hallucinate numbers. Benefits calculations must be exact. Claude is the brain, code is the calculator.

Coverage

  • 24 benefits documented with complete 2026 thresholds (national + Amsterdam municipal)
  • 20 document types classified (CJIB, Belastingdienst, gemeente, DUO, SVB, CAK, energy, incasso, health insurer)
  • Structured output with Zod schema validation - type-safe extraction, not free-form text
  • Security: prompt injection defence in document reader (untrusted user input)

Stack

  • React 19 + Vite + Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui (frontend)
  • Express + Firebase Admin + Cloud Run (backend)
  • Claude API with Vision (document processing)
  • Firebase Auth + Firestore (user data)
  • GitHub Actions (CI/CD auto-deploy)
